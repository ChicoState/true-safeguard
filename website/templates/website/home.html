About

True Safeguard is a platform that helps parents, educators, and individuals identify and understand potentially unsafe media, trends, and online content for the safety of their children. Our mission is to make them aware of certain media and online trends, by providing a platform to learn about recent media and online trends that may be harmful to children. We aim to empower users with knowledge and resources to make informed decisions about the media their children consume. With the help of our platform and the community, we can create a safer online environment for children and promote responsible media consumption.
